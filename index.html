Sharpen Your School’s Competitive Edge

With more sophisticated integration, FACTS and RenWeb simplify your school processes for both parents and front-office administrators. Together, FACTS and RenWeb are changing the way your school operates, for the better.

Payments

The Past

Billing and payments occur in separate systems

Previously, your Student Information System (SIS) and financial management systems operated disparately, keeping payment profiles, bills, and invoices separated. This creates repetitive billing for schools and incompatible experiences for parents.

The Future

A harmonious payment system that works with you

FACTS and RenWeb have created a secure information pathway that updates in real-time. Payment profiles are stored in both systems so you can send a complete bill from either system and parents can pay their way.

Login

The Past

Parents manage a multitude of logins

Throughout every part of the education journey, parents seem to have to manage a new login that makes access to important information unreasonably complicated

The Future

Untangled the experience with one, easy login

With the further integration of the FACTS and RenWeb systems, parents need just one login to access all of their student’s information, from attendance records to payment plan schedules.

Database Management

The Past

Two information databases require your attention

For every charge or student demographic change, your office has to make updates to two different databases, expanding the opportunity for human error and doubling your efforts when it comes to data entry.

The Future

One database, immediately updated

Our systems talk to each other behind the scenes, eliminating dual entry by updating information in both systems every time a change is made.

The Best of Software

The Past

Frankenstein multiple systems for the best package

To find the best software for each portion of your school, you must stitch together incongruent systems and put in extra effort to make them work together.

The Future

The best systems, combined

RenWeb’s top-performing Student Information System integrates so smoothly with the industry-leading tuition management software of FACTS that you won’t need to stitch together any other tool to ensure the success of your school.
